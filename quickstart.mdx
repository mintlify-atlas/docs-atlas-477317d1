## Buyer Quickstart

Start supporting fundraisers in just a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for an account">
    Navigate to the login page and sign in with your Google account. CURaise uses Supabase authentication for secure, passwordless login.
    
    <Note>
      After signing in, you'll be automatically redirected to your buyer dashboard.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse available fundraisers">
    Go to the Browse page to see all active fundraising campaigns. You can search for specific organizations or causes using the search bar.
    
    Each fundraiser displays:
    - Organization name and description
    - Campaign details and goals
    - Available items and pricing
    - Pickup event information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add items to your cart">
    Click on a fundraiser to view its details and browse available items. Add products to your cart by selecting quantities for the items you want to purchase.
    
    <Note>
      Your cart is automatically saved to your browser's local storage, so you won't lose items if you close the tab.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Place your first order">
    Once you've added items to your cart, proceed to checkout. Complete your order by:
    
    1. Reviewing your order items and total
    2. Selecting a pickup event (time and location)
    3. Confirming your purchase
    
    After placing your order, you'll see it appear in your buyer dashboard under "Active" orders.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track your order">
    Monitor your order status from the buyer dashboard:
    
    - **Active**: Orders that haven't been picked up yet
    - **Completed**: Orders you've already collected
    
    You'll receive notifications about your order status and pickup reminders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Seller Quickstart

Create and manage your fundraising organization.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for an account">
    Navigate to the login page and sign in with your Google account. CURaise uses Supabase authentication for secure access.
    
    <Note>
      You can switch between buyer and seller portals at any time. Your account works for both.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your organization">
    From the seller dashboard, click "Create Organization" to get started.
    
    You'll need to provide:
    
    - **Basic Information**: Organization name, description, and contact details
    - **Admin Users**: Email addresses of team members who will help manage the organization
    - **Review**: Confirm all information before submitting
    
    <Note>
      Organizations require authorization. Your organization will need to be approved before you can create fundraisers.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your first fundraiser">
    Once your organization is approved, navigate to your organization page and click "Create Fundraiser".
    
    Configure your fundraiser:
    
    1. **Basic Info**: Campaign name, description, start/end dates
    2. **Add Items**: Products you're selling with prices and descriptions
    3. **Pickup Events**: When and where customers can collect their orders
    4. **Payment Info**: Venmo details for order payments
    5. **Review**: Preview your fundraiser before publishing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch and share your campaign">
    After creating your fundraiser, it will appear on the public browse page for buyers to discover. Share the direct link with your community to start receiving orders.
    
    You can:
    - Post announcements to update supporters
    - Monitor order statistics in real-time
    - Manage pickup events and schedules
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage incoming orders">
    Track all orders from the seller orders page. You'll see:
    
    - Order details and customer information
    - Payment status (pending, completed, refunded)
    - Pickup event assignments
    - Order fulfillment status
    
    <Note>
      Orders can be filtered by fundraiser, payment status, and pickup event to help you stay organized.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
