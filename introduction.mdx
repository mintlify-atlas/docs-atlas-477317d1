  <Steps>
    <Step title="Create an account">
      Sign up for a CURaise account using your email address. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
      
      ```bash
      # Access the platform at
      https://curaise.netlify.app/login
      ```
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Choose your role">
      After signing in, decide whether you're a buyer looking to support fundraisers or a seller managing an organization.
      
      <Note>
        Buyers can browse and purchase from fundraisers immediately. Sellers need to set up an organization first.
      </Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Browse or create fundraisers">
      **As a buyer:** Navigate to `/buyer/browse` to discover active fundraisers in your community.
      
      **As a seller:** Create your first fundraiser at `/seller/fundraiser/new` after setting up your organization.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Place or manage orders">
      **Buyers** can add items to their cart, checkout with Venmo or other payment methods, and track orders.
      
      **Sellers** can view incoming orders, verify payments, and manage pickup events from the seller dashboard.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
